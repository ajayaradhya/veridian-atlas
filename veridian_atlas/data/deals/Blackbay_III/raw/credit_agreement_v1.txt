CREDIT AGREEMENT
BLACKBAY III HOLDINGS

Effective Date: January 12, 2024
Document Version: v1

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 – DEFINITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
1.1 "Borrower" refers to Blackbay III Holdings LLC.
1.2 "Lender" refers to North Meridian Capital.
1.3 "Revolving Credit Facility" refers to the facility described in Section 2.4.
1.4 "Term Loan A Facility" refers to the facility described in Section 2.10.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 – LOAN FACILITIES
------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 Purpose
The purpose of this Agreement is to provide financing to the Borrower for general operations,
debt refinancing, and related corporate activities.

2.4 Revolving Credit Facility
The Lender agrees to provide a Revolving Credit Facility of up to USD 50,000,000 with an
interest rate of LIBOR + 2.0%. Drawdowns may occur at any time subject to covenants.

2.10 Maturity Date (IMPORTANT)
The Term Loan A Facility shall mature on December 31, 2027, at which time all outstanding
principal, accrued interest and other amounts become due in full.

The Revolving Credit Facility shall mature on December 31, 2026, unless terminated earlier
under the provisions of Section 8.2.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 – INTEREST & PAYMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 Interest Accrual
Interest for both facilities will be calculated daily based on actual/360 convention.

3.4 Payment Schedule
Interest payments are due quarterly. Principal is due at maturity unless accelerated.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 – FEES
------------------------------------------------------------
Upfront fee: 0.75% of committed Term Loan A amount.
Commitment fee: 0.30% per annum of undrawn Revolving balance.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5 – COLLATERAL & SECURITY
------------------------------------------------------------
The Borrower's assets are pledged as collateral. Guarantees are provided by Blackbay Parent Co.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 – COVENANTS
------------------------------------------------------------
6.1 Reporting
Borrower must provide quarterly financial statements within 45 days of period end.

6.3 Leverage Ratios
Maximum total leverage ratio: 4.25x trailing twelve months EBITDA.

6.5 Additional Debt
Borrower may not incur new senior indebtedness without lender consent.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 – EVENTS OF DEFAULT
------------------------------------------------------------
Material covenant breach, non-payment, fraud, bankruptcy events, or breach of guarantees
constitute events of default, enabling acceleration of the loan.

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 – TERMINATION
------------------------------------------------------------
8.2 Early Termination
The Lender may terminate the Revolving facility early upon breach of Sections 6 or 7.

------------------------------------------------------------
END OF DOCUMENT
