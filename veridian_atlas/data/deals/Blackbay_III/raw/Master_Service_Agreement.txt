SECTION 1 – Scope of Engagement
Blackbay III Holdings ("Client") engages Veridian Atlas Partners ("Service Provider") to perform data infrastructure, risk-model deployment, and analytics support across Client’s portfolio companies. Services include: (i) asset-level data ingestion, (ii) modeling pipeline deployment, and (iii) reporting integration into existing finance systems.

1.1 Responsibilities (Client)
Client shall provide system access credentials, environment endpoints, and non-public datasets required for service delivery.

1.2 Responsibilities (Service Provider)
Service Provider shall deploy infrastructure components, perform testing, and deliver production integration within the agreed commercial timeframe.

SECTION 2 – Term & Termination
2.1 Term
This Agreement remains effective for twenty-four (24) months from the Effective Date unless terminated earlier.

2.2 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate with thirty (30) days’ written notice if the counterparty materially breaches obligations.

SECTION 3 – Reporting & Audit
3.1 Performance Reporting
Monthly performance reports shall be provided including uptime %, data latency KPIs, exception logs, and SLA breaches.

3.3 Audit Rights
Client may request a compliance audit not more than twice annually.
