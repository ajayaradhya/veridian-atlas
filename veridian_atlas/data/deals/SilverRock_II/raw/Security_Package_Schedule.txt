SECTION 1 – Collateral Components
1.1 Primary Collateral
Mortgage charge over revenue-producing assets located in Bengaluru, Pune, and Hyderabad.

1.2 Secondary Collateral
Pledge of receivables, reserve accounts, and escrow-controlled operating accounts.

SECTION 2 – Enforcement Framework
2.1 Trigger Events
Failure to satisfy covenant tests for 2 consecutive quarters constitutes an Enforcement Trigger.

2.3 Enforcement Mechanics
Upon trigger: cash sweeps activated, disbursement controls initiated, asset sales prioritized by liquidity yield.

SECTION 3 – Monitoring & Controls
3.2 Quarterly Reporting
Reports include asset cashflow summaries, impairment tests, LTV updates, and asset-level breakage reports.
