SECTION 1 – Required Before First Draw
1.1 Documentation Delivery
Executed agreements, KYC packages, AML confirmations, and board resolutions required.

1.2 Legal Opinions
External counsel must issue enforceability opinions on cross-border treatment of pledged assets.

SECTION 2 – Financial Conditions
2.3 Covenant Certification
Minimum DSCR: 1.25x
Minimum Interest Coverage Ratio (ICR): 2.10x
Max Leverage: 3.8x Net Debt / EBITDA

2.4 Portfolio Concentration Limits
Single obligor exposure may not exceed 21% of aggregate facility value.

SECTION 3 – Operational Conditions
3.1 System Integration
API linkage between Axiom Capital treasury systems and Veridian Atlas reporting rails must be tested before drawdown.

3.4 Data Room Requirements
Virtual data room must remain accessible to auditors for 36 months post-drawdown.
