SECTION 1 – Defined Terms (General)
"Asset Pool" refers to the aggregated set of real estate or credit assets pledged under this Agreement.

1.1 "Operating Covenant"
Minimum DSCR of 1.20x must be maintained across the pooled asset cash flows.

1.2 "Strategic Event"
Any sale, merger, recapitalization, asset disposal (> 8% NAV), or restructuring requiring consent.

SECTION 2 – Material Terms
2.3 "Eligible Collateral"
Assets meeting: (i) audit-verified book value, (ii) enforceable ownership rights, (iii) liquidation horizon < 14 months.

2.4 "Restricted Party"
Entities under sanction lists, AML-flagged entities, or beneficial owners obscured through undisclosed nominee arrangements.

SECTION 3 – Measurement Standards
3.1 Valuation Method
NAV shall be measured quarterly using discounted future cash flows with a 10.5% hurdle.
