SECTION 1 – Fee Structure Overview
Fees are structured as a hybrid subscription + usage variable model. All fees are billed in INR.

1.1 Base Subscription
Monthly Platform Access Fee: INR 5,50,000.

1.2 Variable Usage Fees
Compute Execution Charges (per pipeline run): INR 4,500.
Storage Allocation (per TB/month): INR 11,000.

SECTION 2 – Invoicing & Payment
2.1 Payment Terms
Payment due within fifteen (15) calendar days of invoice date. Late payments accrue interest @ 1.5% per month.

2.2 Currency & Taxes
All taxes, GST obligations, and withholding remittances are the responsibility of the Client.

SECTION 3 – Penalties & Adjustments
3.1 Service Downtime
If availability falls below 99.3% uptime per calendar month, a 12% service credit shall be issued.

3.4 Termination Balance
Outstanding fees become immediately payable upon termination.
